Portrait Lighting Techniques.

Rembrandt lighting, named after the painter, is known for the triangle of light created on one cheek.
Not quite the same as loop lighting, rembradnt lighting lights up one side of the face more than another.

Loop lighting creates a small shadow of a subject's nose on their cheeks.

Butterfly lighting creates a butterfly shaped shadow under the nose.

Split lighting splits the face in half; one side lit up, the other in shadow.
